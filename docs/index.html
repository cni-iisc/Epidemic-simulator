The IISc-TIFR City-Scale Agent Based SimulatorÂ¶

The agent based city-scale simulator is a generic multi-agent simulator which is developed by the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru and the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai to study the effects of different non-pharmaceutical interventions on spread of the CoVID-19 infection in a city-scale.
